Controversies & Storylines

PPL 2.0 wasn’t just football — it was chaos, drama, transfers, ego battles, and clutch storylines that shaped the entire season.

1. The R9 Transfer Earthquake

Pakistani Lions entered Season 2 as defending champions — until their midfield engine R9 left to join Hamari FC. He became Midfielder of the Season, but the gold slipped away. A dynasty shaken.

2. Basho vs Navyan — The Rivalry

Season 1’s superstar Basho entered PPL 2.0 with unmatched confidence. Southall’s captain Navyan responded with a cold “We’ll see about that.” In the semifinals, Southall eliminated Basho’s Lions — ending their era.

3. The German Goalkeeper Masterstroke

Just before the semifinals, Southall signed a German goalkeeper who became the backbone of their title run. Clutch saves, penalty heroics, and ice-cold composure — a season-defining signing.
